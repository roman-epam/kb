BLIS & NA Business Unit

Our space dedicated to sharing insights about our Business Information & Learning Services (BLIS) Business Unit, abbreviated as BLIS BU. Our Unit represents an integration of business domains and geographical locations in the North America region, operating under the adept leadership of Manager, Oleg Vilchinsky.

Our prime objective is to fortify the connection between individuals and our business by empowering them to exchange knowledge, experience, and the wide range of opportunities that our network offers.

Major industries we work in

Business Information Services

Business Information Clients use Information as a foundation of their Business. Legal Information, Economical, Financial, Tax and Market Data, Healthcare and Life Sciences Information, Educational Content, Print and Digital Media are just a few examples.

Education & Learning Services

In 2019 East3 BU was joined by Competentum to expand EPAM's digital education and learning capabilities. Now our E&L experts provide a complete service proposition for education vertical and EPAM’s enterprise clients looking to accelerate their transformations. We offer a unique combination of capabilities – education technology, learning content, and experience design – that no other company in EPAM’s category provides. ​

Scientific Content Development & Curation

Scalable 250-person technical team with about 60% PhD, MD or Dr. Sci. data curators.​ Consultants from different fields of knowledge, effective system of training, close collaboration with software engineering and algorithm development groups.
